Brick Smasher

Overview of Gameplay

The information below may only be applicable to the latest version.

Bricks

In the current version of BrickSmasher there are 10 levels and 10 different types of bricks. Here's what they are (Spoiler Alert!):

Simple Brick - A normal brick that takes 1 hit to destroy. Introduced on level 1. Game ID: "brick_1".

Tougher Brick - A tougher brick that takes 2 hit to destroy. Introduced on level 2. Game ID: "brick_2".

Toughest Brick - The toughest brick that takes 3 hit to destroy. Introduced on level 3. Game ID: "brick_3".

Steel Brick - A steel brick that practically can't be destroyed. Introduced on level 4. Game ID: "steel_brick".

Bomb (small) - A bomb that damages (by 1 hit) 4 bricks immediately around it. Introduced on level 5. Game ID: "bomb_1".

Bouncy Brick - A bouncy brick that takes 1 hit to destroy and makes the ball faster. Introduced on level 6. Game ID: "bouncy_brick".

Harmless Troll Brick - A troll brick that takes 1 hit to destroy and makes the ball go on a weird trajectory. Introduced on level 7. Game ID: "troll_brick_2".

Spooky Skeleton Brick - A brick that takes 1 hit to destroy and plays music. Introduced on level 8. Game ID: "spooky_brick".

Bomb (big) - A bomb that damages (by 1 hit) 12 bricks around it. Introduced on level 10. Game ID: "bomb_2".

Troll Troll Brick - A troll brick that takes 1 hit to destroy YOU. Introduced on level 10. Game ID: "troll_brick_1".

Controls

Here are the controls, excluding some 5 hack-keys:

Arrow keys to move the pad,

Space key to lauch ball,

P to pause,

ESC to return to menu,

G to skip level. (If you cheat, your achievements/score won't be saved!)

On menu: arrow keys to change options, space key to select.

About score, this is how it works:

-hitting a block will give you 10 score (damaging blocks with bombs counts as hitting them)

-that amount will increase every level (+10 score on level 1, +20 on level 2, +30 on level 3, etc.)

-you lose 1 score every ~2 seconds (depends on lag)
